Welcome

Clint Mooney is an independent web developer and aspiring game designer. Currently, he is attending Gardner-Webb University as a Computer Science and Philosophy/Theology double major. He dabbles in pixel art when he isn't playing Dungeons & Dragons or some other RPG.

He is most interested in creating a series of Christian video games inspired by the style of CS Lewis and J. R. R. Tolkein. Clint is firmly of the opinion that gamers as a whole are a unreached people group and are utterly neglected and looked down upon by the global church.
